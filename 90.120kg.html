First month

BREAKFAST:
Approximately 45 g of fresh cheddar cheese, tomatoes and peppers on whole wheat bread, without butter. 5 saddle olives Seasonal fresh raw vegetables

Snack

2 cucumbers

Lunch


1 bowl of lentil soup with vegetables
5-6 stuffed cabbage rolls prepared with bulgur and meat
1 bowl of skim-free yoghurt
Evening meal
4-5 tablespoons of bulgur pilaf with green lentils
1 large bowl of purslane salad with yoghurt

Snack


1 glass of kefir
6 raw almonds

Second month

MORNING:
Drink a glass of warm water on an empty stomach

Breakfast

1 slice whole wheat bread
1 egg, curd cheese omelette flavored with dill and parsley
1 slice of pineapple
Seasonal raw vegetables

Snack

10 green plums

Lunch

150 grams of steamed fish cooked with plenty of vegetables,
Arugula salad with cherry tomatoes, 1 teaspoon of olive oil and squeezed lemon
1 slice whole wheat bread

Evening meal

Steamed broccoli, brussels sprouts and cauliflower (can be enriched with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, plenty of lemon and spices to suit your taste).
1 bowl of mint tzatziki made with plain yoghurt
1 slice whole wheat bread
